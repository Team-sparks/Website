Paper Presentation


Event Description

Paper presentation is an individual competition where each participant is required to make a paper about a given topic or area, and then present it in front of the juries.

Here are the Topics for the Event
 • Cryptography  • IOT
 • Cloud computing  • Optical Current Transformer (OCT)
 • Artificial intelligence  • Digital manufacturing
 • Data warehousing  • Advanced wireless communication
 • Nano technology  • Building information modelling (BIM)

Rules

• It can be participated in team of two or as an individual.

• Abstract of the topic should be sent via E-Mail to a day prior to the event.

• IEEE format is a compulsion for the paper.

• Abstract should contain your information (Name, Phone no., College)

 

Quizzy


Event Description

A quiz is a form of game or mind sport in which players attempt to answer questions correctly about a certain or variety of subjects.

Quizzes may be held on a variety of subjects or subject-specific. The format of the quiz can also vary.

Rules

• An event involving of individual participant and the event consisting of two rounds (Prelim and Main) 

• The top scorer contestants of Prelim round will be qualified for the Main round. 

• Main round consists of sub rounds, the participant with participation and top scorer in maximum rounds will be the winner. 

• The main round will be hosted through online meeting apps, so participants are suggested to have stable internet connection. 

• Any participant or all spectating throughout the event will win exciting prizes at the end of the event.

 

Digital Poster Presentation


Event Description

A poster presentation is a way to communicate your research or your understanding of a topic in a short and concise format.

You will need to analyse and evaluate information, synthesise ideas and creatively demonstrate your understanding of a topic or the findings of your research.

Poster presentations develop your ability to communicate perceptively and concisely - an important workplace skill

Rules

• The team can comprise of two participants.

• The topics will be given few minutes prior to the competition.

• It’s a time-based event, so participants are suggested to be punctual.

• Judgement depends on the quality of design and presentation of the poster.
