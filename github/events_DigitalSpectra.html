Digital Spectra 2021

Spectra is an event conducted by team SPARK every year in the college premises. Since we all are fighting this pandemic, SPARK ensures not to gap their tradition and to unstop the opportunity for all the young budding individuals, the Spectra will go online this year. This experience has made us to take a step forward to redefine, rearrange and restart the things with the time. So, this year the team SPARK brings you “DIGITAL SPECTRA”, a step taken forward to make the things more knowledgeable, more interesting and more joyful through the screens of your gadgets. A chain of events, from adding knowledge to your forum to bringing out the joy through fun and artistic stuff....

This year due to pandemic, we were unable to have the Spectra on campus. This is the reason for the rise of Digital Spectra. Digital Spectra is an full fledged event that is being conducted online. This time the students of spark have decided to expand the idea of the event Spectra to all the other institutes to share the platform of opportunities. This time the students will be able to participate in different kind of competitions such as Paper Presentation, Digital Poster presentation, Technical Quiz and a Fun-Tastic event. For more details and registrations please visit our events page.
